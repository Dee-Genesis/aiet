Programme Overview

A Foundation Built for Professional Success

The Certificate Programs at AIET are designed to provide high-quality, focused, and career-oriented education within the field of Education and Training. These programmes serve as a foundational pathway for individuals seeking to develop essential teaching, training, and instructional skills.

Structured to address the growing global demand for qualified educators, trainers, and education support professionals, each certificate integrates core educational theories with hands-on application — enabling learners to develop real-world skills through lesson planning, instructional delivery, case studies, and reflective practice.

All programmes are progressive and stackable, allowing graduates to build upon their certificates and advance into Diploma, Higher Diploma, and Postgraduate Diploma qualifications within AIET.

Programme Structure

Structure & Key Information

Everything you need to know about eligibility, delivery, duration, and credits.

✅

Eligibility Requirements

  • Minimum high school diploma or equivalent qualification
  • Basic proficiency in English (reading and writing)
  • Demonstrated interest in education, teaching, or training
  • No prior teaching experience required — though advantageous
🖥️

Mode of Study

  • Online Learning: Fully remote access to lectures, materials, and assessments
  • Blended Learning: Online study combined with guided academic support
  • Self-Paced Learning: Flexible schedule within a structured completion period
⏱️

Programme Duration

  • Short Programmes: 4 – 6 weeks
  • Standard Programmes: 8 – 12 weeks
  • Extended Programmes: 3 – 6 months
Short Standard Extended
🎓

Credit Unit System

  • 1 Credit Unit = 10–15 learning hours
  • Short Programmes: 3 Credits
  • Standard Programmes: 6 Credits
  • Extended Programmes: 9–12 Credits

Assessment Framework

How You Are Evaluated

A balanced, continuous assessment model that values both theoretical understanding and practical application.

3-Part
Assessment Balanced Approach
Continuous Assessment
40%
Practical / Skills-Based
40%
Final Assessment
20%

Continuous Assessment (40%)

  • Short assignments and coursework
  • Quizzes and knowledge checks
  • Class participation and guided activities
  • Practical exercises and task-based learning

Practical / Skills-Based Assessment (40%)

  • Practical tasks and demonstrations
  • Mini projects or applied exercises
  • Classroom simulations or teaching practice
  • Core professional skills demonstration

Final Assessment (20%)

  • Short written examination
  • Final project or practical demonstration
  • Overall programme content evaluation
Grading System

Award Classifications

AIET utilises a structured grading framework that evaluates performance across four classifications.

A
Distinction
70% – 100%
Excellent performance with strong understanding, critical thinking, and application of knowledge.
B
Merit
60% – 69%
Good performance with clear understanding and effective application of key concepts.
C
Pass
50% – 59%
Satisfactory performance meeting the minimum academic and practical requirements.
F
Fail
Below 50%
Does not meet required standards. Reassessment opportunity may be available subject to policy.

Certificates are awarded as Certificate with Distinction, Certificate with Merit, or Certificate (Pass). A minimum grade of Pass (C) is required for completion.
