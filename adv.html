Programme Overview

Lead. Innovate. Transform Education.

The Higher/Advanced Diploma Programs at AIET represent an advanced level of professional qualification designed to develop specialized expertise, leadership capability, and strategic competence within the field of education and training.

These programs build on foundational and intermediate knowledge acquired at Certificate and Diploma levels, offering learners a deeper and more critical understanding of educational theories, pedagogical practices, curriculum innovation, policy frameworks, and research methodologies. The curriculum reflects contemporary developments in education and prepares learners for the evolving demands of modern teaching, training, and academic leadership.

A key feature of these programs is their emphasis on independent learning, critical analysis, and applied research. Learners engage with real-world educational challenges, evaluate current practices, and develop innovative solutions within their professional contexts — ensuring graduates can contribute meaningfully to institutional improvement and educational advancement.

Programme Structure

Structure & Key Information

Everything you need to know about eligibility, delivery, duration, and credits.

✅

Eligibility Requirements

  • A Diploma in Education and Training or related qualification (strongly recommended)
  • Or equivalent academic qualification and/or relevant professional experience in education or training
  • Proficiency in the English language (written and spoken)
  • Demonstrated commitment to professional development in education and training
  • Experienced professionals without formal qualifications assessed on a case-by-case basis
🖥️

Mode of Study

  • Online Learning: Full digital access to lectures, materials, and assessments
  • Blended Learning: Combination of online study with structured academic guidance
  • Guided Learning: Instructor-led sessions, mentoring, and academic supervision
  • Flexible Learning: Structured timelines with adaptable study schedules
⏱️

Programme Duration

  • Standard Duration: 9 – 12 months
  • Extended Duration: Up to 18 months (depending on study pace and specialization)
Standard Extended
🎓

Credit Unit Structure

  • 1 Credit Unit = 10–15 learning hours
  • Total Credit Range: 36–48 Credits
  • Credits include lectures, independent study, research, applied projects, and professional practice activities
U.S.-Style Framework Research-Intensive

Assessment Framework

How You Are Evaluated

An advanced, analytical, and research-driven assessment model that evaluates theoretical knowledge, practical competence, and professional application at the highest level.

3-Part
Assessment Advanced Level

Continuous Assessment (30%)

  • Module-based assignments and academic coursework
  • Case studies and scenario-based strategic analysis
  • Reflective journals and professional practice logs
  • Class participation and guided learning activities

Applied / Professional Project (40%)

  • Applied project or professional practice report
  • Instructional design or programme development project
  • Workplace-based or case-based project (where applicable)
  • Focuses on practical application, problem-solving, and professional innovation

Final Assessment (30%)

  • Written examination
  • Project presentation or professional portfolio review
  • Capstone project or dissertation (where applicable)
Grading System

Award Classifications

At Higher/Advanced Diploma level, students are expected to demonstrate advanced theoretical knowledge, critical thinking, and professional leadership competence.

A
Distinction
70% – 100%
Exceptional performance with advanced strategic thinking, leadership insight, and research-driven application of knowledge.
B
Merit
60% – 69%
Strong performance with clear mastery of advanced theories and effective professional application in educational contexts.
C
Pass
50% – 59%
Satisfactory performance meeting the minimum academic, practical, and professional competency requirements at this level.
F
Fail
Below 50%
Does not meet required standards. Reassessment, resubmission, or revision of research projects may be available subject to policy.

Awards are issued as Higher/Advanced Diploma with Distinction, Higher/Advanced Diploma with Merit, or Higher/Advanced Diploma (Pass). A minimum grade of Pass (C) is required, along with completion of all modules, assessments, research components, and the capstone project or dissertation (where applicable).
