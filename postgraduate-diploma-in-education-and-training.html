Programme Overview

The Highest Standard of Professional Excellence.

The Postgraduate Diploma Programs at AIET represent the highest level of professional qualification offered within the institute — designed for individuals seeking advanced academic, research, and leadership expertise in education and training.

These programs are intended for graduates and experienced professionals aiming to develop specialized knowledge, strategic insight, and high-level competencies required for senior roles in education, training, policy development, and academic research. The curriculum integrates advanced concepts in educational theory, leadership, curriculum innovation, research methodology, and evidence-based practice.

With a strong focus on critical thinking, independent research, and professional leadership, the Postgraduate Diploma enables learners to evaluate existing educational systems, design innovative solutions, and contribute meaningfully to the advancement of teaching and learning globally. AIET also recognizes Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL), allowing experienced professionals without formal qualifications to be considered.

Programme Structure

Structure & Key Information

Eligibility, delivery, duration, and credits at a glance.

✅

Eligibility Requirements

  • A Higher/Advanced Diploma, Bachelor's degree, or equivalent qualification in education or a related discipline
  • Or a recognized professional qualification with extensive work experience in teaching, training, or educational leadership
  • Demonstrated proficiency in English (academic reading and writing)
  • Evidence of readiness for advanced study, critical analysis, and independent research
  • RPL available for experienced professionals without formal academic qualifications
🖥️

Mode of Study

  • Online Learning: Comprehensive digital platform with access to lectures, research materials, and academic journals
  • Blended Learning: Independent study combined with structured academic engagement
  • Guided Learning: Academic supervision, mentoring, and dedicated research support
  • Research-Led Learning: Integration of independent and supervised research throughout the programme
⏱️

Programme Duration

  • Standard Duration: 12 – 18 months
  • Extended Duration: Up to 24 months (depending on research scope and learner pace)
Standard Extended
🎓

Credit Unit Structure

  • 1 Credit Unit = 10–15 learning hours
  • Total Credit Requirement: 60–90 Credits
  • Credits cover lectures, independent study, research activities, academic writing, and dissertation work

The Dissertation

Your Original Contribution to Educational Knowledge

The dissertation is the centrepiece of the Postgraduate Diploma — a substantial independent research project that demonstrates your ability to identify a research problem, engage with scholarly literature, collect and analyse data, and present findings with rigour and originality.

Learners are supported throughout by dedicated academic supervisors who provide guidance on methodology, academic writing, and research ethics. The final dissertation is presented and defended before an academic panel.

01

Identify a Research Problem

Define a meaningful, original research question rooted in a real educational challenge or knowledge gap.

02

Literature Review & Framework

Critically evaluate existing academic literature and establish a theoretical framework for your study.

03

Data Collection & Analysis

Apply appropriate research methodologies to collect, analyse, and interpret your data with rigour and ethics.

04

Present Findings & Defend

Submit your dissertation and defend your research findings and recommendations before an academic panel.

Grading System

Award Classifications

At Postgraduate Diploma level, students are expected to demonstrate advanced theoretical insight, original research, and scholarly excellence throughout their programme.

A
Distinction
70% – 100%
Exceptional scholarly performance with original thinking, advanced research quality, and outstanding professional application.
B
Merit
60% – 69%
Strong academic performance demonstrating mastery of advanced concepts and effective research and analysis.
C
Pass
50% – 59%
Satisfactory performance meeting the minimum postgraduate academic, research, and professional competency standards.
F
Fail
Below 50%
Does not meet required postgraduate standards. Assignment resubmission or revision of research work may be available.

Awards are conferred as Postgraduate Diploma with Distinction, Postgraduate Diploma with Merit, or Postgraduate Diploma (Pass). Students must achieve a minimum of Pass (C) in all modules, complete all coursework, and successfully submit and defend their dissertation or research project.
