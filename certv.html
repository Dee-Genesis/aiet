⚠️ Important — Scope of Verification

Certificate verification confirms authenticity only. It does not guarantee employment, grant professional licensing, or replace independent evaluation by employers or institutions. Recognition may vary by country, employer, or regulatory requirements.

Policies & Protections

Fraud Prevention, Data Protection & Guidelines

🚨

Fraud Prevention & Reporting

AIET maintains a strict stance against academic fraud. If a fraudulent certificate is suspected, report it to info@aietglobal.us with all supporting details. AIET will investigate and take appropriate administrative or legal action, including invalidating the certificate.

🔒

Data Protection & Confidentiality

All verification requests are handled in a secure and confidential manner. Only necessary information is disclosed for verification purposes, and personal data is never shared beyond the scope of the request. All processing aligns with applicable data protection standards.

📌

Important Guidelines

Only certificates issued directly by AIET are eligible for verification. All requests must be submitted through official channels. AIET reserves the right to decline incomplete or suspicious requests and to request additional information. Any tampering or falsification renders a certificate invalid.
