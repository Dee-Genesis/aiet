General Information

5 Questions
What is the American Institute of Education and Training (AIET)?
+

The American Institute of Education and Training (AIET) is a United States-based educational and professional training institution dedicated to delivering high-quality, flexible, and career-oriented learning opportunities to a diverse global population. Established to bridge the gap between academic knowledge and practical workforce skills, AIET serves as a platform for individuals seeking to enhance their competencies, advance their careers, and adapt to the evolving demands of the modern workplace.

AIET is legally registered in the United States and recognized by the Secretary of State of Arizona as a duly registered entity authorized to operate. The institute specializes in Certificate, Diploma, Higher/Advanced Diploma, and Postgraduate Diploma programs focused on education and training.

Where is AIET based and where does it operate?
+

AIET is registered in the United States with its official registered address at 1950 W Corporate Way #15593, Anaheim, CA 92801. As an online and distance learning institution, AIET operates globally — welcoming students from all countries across Africa, Asia, Europe, the Americas, and beyond.

There is no geographic restriction on enrollment. All programs are delivered digitally, so students can study from anywhere in the world with an internet connection.

What types of programs does AIET offer?
+

AIET offers a comprehensive suite of professional qualifications structured as a progressive learning pathway:

LevelDurationCredits
Certificate Programs4 weeks – 6 months3 – 12 credits
Diploma Programs6 – 12 months24 – 36 credits
Higher/Advanced Diploma9 – 18 months36 – 60 credits
Postgraduate Diploma12 – 24 months60 – 90 credits

All programs are concentrated in the field of education and training, spanning areas such as Early Childhood Education, Teacher Training, Educational Leadership, Special Needs Education, Educational Technology, STEM Education, and many more — with over 150 specialization options across all levels.

What is AIET's teaching philosophy and approach?
+

AIET operates on a learner-centered educational model with a strong emphasis on practical, applied learning. The institute integrates core educational theories with real-world case studies, project-based assignments, and industry-relevant scenarios to ensure students develop skills they can immediately use in their professional environments.

AIET's eight core values guide all aspects of its operations:

  • Excellence — Highest academic and service standards
  • Integrity — Honesty, fairness, and ethical conduct
  • Innovation — Modern technology and creative teaching
  • Student-Centered Learning — Students at the heart of everything
  • Diversity & Inclusion — Equal opportunity for all backgrounds
  • Professionalism — Discipline, responsibility, ethical behavior
  • Lifelong Learning — Continuous personal and professional growth
  • Global Perspective — Cross-cultural awareness and adaptability
How do I contact AIET?
+

You can reach AIET through the following channels:

  • 📞 Phone: +1 (480) 955-0876

  • 🏅 Certificate Verification: info@aietglobal.us
  • 🌐 Contact Form: Available on the official website
Our team is available during business hours to respond to inquiries. For verification requests, allow 2–5 working days for processing.

Admissions

6 Questions
What are the general admission requirements?
+

Admission requirements vary by program level:

Program LevelMinimum Requirement
CertificateHigh school diploma or equivalent; basic English proficiency
DiplomaHigh school diploma or equivalent; or AIET Certificate completion
Higher/Advanced DiplomaDiploma in Education or related qualification; or equivalent professional experience
Postgraduate DiplomaHigher/Advanced Diploma, Bachelor's degree, or recognized professional qualification with extensive experience

AIET also recognizes Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL) for the Postgraduate Diploma, allowing experienced professionals without formal qualifications to be considered based on professional achievements and competencies.

Is there an age requirement for admission?
+

Applicants are generally expected to be at least 16 years old for Certificate programs. Higher-level programs (Diploma, Higher Diploma, Postgraduate Diploma) typically require older applicants with appropriate academic backgrounds or relevant professional experience.

Applications may be assessed on a case-by-case basis for applicants who do not meet the standard age or qualification criteria but demonstrate strong professional experience and readiness.

How do I apply and what documents do I need?
+

The application process is straightforward and fully online:

  1. Visit the Application Portal on the AIET website
  2. Complete and submit the online application form
  3. Upload required supporting documents (academic certificates, ID, etc.)
  4. Make the necessary program payment to confirm enrollment
  5. Receive your enrollment confirmation and access credentials
📎 Documents typically required include copies of prior academic certificates, a valid government-issued ID, and any relevant professional certificates for advanced programs.
Can I apply from outside the United States?
+

Yes, absolutely. AIET is a fully online institution and proudly accepts students from all countries worldwide. There are no geographic restrictions on enrollment. AIET actively serves students across Africa, Asia, Europe, the Middle East, and the Americas.

All learning materials, assessments, and communications are delivered digitally, making it easy to study from any location with a reliable internet connection.

Can I enroll without a teaching background?
+

Yes. For Certificate and Diploma programs, prior teaching or training experience is not required but may be an added advantage. These programs are specifically designed for:

  • Aspiring teachers and educators entering the profession for the first time
  • Teaching assistants and education support personnel
  • Professionals transitioning into the education sector from other industries
  • Individuals pursuing continuous professional development

For Higher/Advanced Diploma and Postgraduate Diploma programs, relevant professional experience in education or training is strongly recommended.

Is there a language requirement?
+

All AIET programs are delivered in English. Applicants are required to demonstrate basic proficiency in English for Certificate programs and progressively higher levels of English competency for Diploma and Postgraduate Diploma programs.

For the Postgraduate Diploma, applicants must demonstrate academic English proficiency, including the ability to read, write, and critically engage with academic literature and produce scholarly work in English.

Formal English proficiency tests (such as IELTS or TOEFL) are not currently mandated, but the ability to successfully complete coursework in English is essential.

Programs & Study Mode

6 Questions
How are AIET programs delivered?
+

AIET programs are delivered through flexible, technology-driven formats that accommodate learners in different situations:

  • Online Learning: Fully remote access to lectures, study materials, and assessments via the digital platform
  • Blended Learning: Combination of online learning with structured academic guidance and support
  • Guided Learning: Instructor-led sessions, mentoring, and academic supervision
  • Self-Paced Learning: Flexible study within defined completion timelines

Students have 24/7 access to all course materials, allowing them to study anytime and anywhere at their own convenience.

How long does it take to complete a program?
+

Program durations vary by level and specialization:

Program LevelStandard DurationMaximum Duration
Certificate (Short)4 – 6 weeks—
Certificate (Standard)8 – 12 weeks—
Certificate (Extended)3 – 6 months—
Diploma6 – 9 months12 months
Higher/Advanced Diploma9 – 12 months18 months

AIET's flexible model allows students to study at their own pace within these defined timelines, making it easy to balance education with work and personal commitments.

How many specializations are available and how do I choose one?
+

AIET offers over 150 specialization options across all program levels, organized into the following broad categories:

  • General Education and Training
  • Early Childhood Education and Care
  • Teacher Training and Pedagogy
  • Educational Leadership and Administration
  • Curriculum and Instructional Design
  • Special Needs and Inclusive Education
  • Educational Technology and E-Learning
  • Educational Psychology and Counseling
  • Adult and Continuing Education
  • Language Education and Literacy
  • STEM Education and Innovation
  • Assessment, Testing, and Evaluation
  • Training and Development (Professional Education)

Choose a specialization aligned with your current career, teaching subject area, or professional aspirations. If you are unsure, contact our admissions team for personalized guidance.

Can I study while working full-time?
+

Yes. AIET programs are specifically designed with working professionals in mind. The self-paced and flexible study options allow you to manage your studies around your employment schedule. You can:

  • Access all materials at any time — morning, evening, or weekends
  • Set your own study pace within the allowed program timeline
  • Complete assignments and assessments at your convenience

Many AIET graduates have successfully completed their programs while working full-time in schools, corporate training environments, and other professional settings.

Can I progress from one qualification level to a higher one?
+

Yes. AIET programs are designed to be progressive and stackable, forming a clear academic and professional pathway:

  • Certificate → Diploma in Education and Training
  • Diploma → Higher/Advanced Diploma Programs
  • Higher/Advanced Diploma → Postgraduate Diploma Programs
  • Postgraduate Diploma → Master's Degree Programs (at partner institutions)

This progressive structure ensures that your qualifications build meaningfully on each other, supporting continuous career and academic advancement.

What technology or equipment do I need to study online?
+

To study with AIET, you will need:

  • A computer, laptop, tablet, or smartphone
  • A reliable internet connection
  • Basic digital literacy — ability to navigate online platforms, submit documents, and communicate via email

No specialist software is required. All course materials are accessible through AIET's online learning platform. Technical support is available if you encounter any access or platform issues.

Assessment & Grading

5 Questions
How are students assessed at AIET?
+

Assessment varies by program level and is designed to be comprehensive, continuous, and competency-based:

ComponentCertificateDiploma / Higher DiplomaPostgraduate Diploma
Continuous Assessment40%30%20%
Practical / Applied Project40%40%30%
Final Assessment / Thesis20%30%50%

Assessment methods include written assignments, case studies, lesson planning tasks, projects, research work, reflective journals, and final examinations or dissertation defense (Postgraduate level).

What grading system does AIET use?
+

AIET uses the following standard grading classification across all program levels:

Score RangeGradeClassification
70% – 100%ADistinction
60% – 69%BMerit
50% – 59%CPass
Below 50%FFail

Final certificates and diplomas are issued with the classification earned: with Distinction, with Merit, or Pass.

What is the minimum score required to pass a program?
+

To successfully complete any AIET program, students must achieve a minimum overall grade of Pass (C) — 50% or above across all assessments and modules.

For Postgraduate Diploma programs specifically, students must also:

  • Achieve at least a Pass (C) in all individual modules
  • Successfully complete and submit the mandatory dissertation or major research project
  • Demonstrate advanced professional and academic competence
What happens if I fail an assessment or module?
+

Students who do not meet the required standards are given opportunities to improve their performance through AIET's reassessment policy:

  • Resubmit assignments — submit revised or improved work
  • Retake assessments — undertake a second attempt at tests or examinations
  • Revise and resubmit research projects — applicable at Higher Diploma and Postgraduate level

All reassessment opportunities are subject to institutional policies and applicable timelines. Students are encouraged to seek academic support before and after assessments to maximize their chances of success.

Will I receive feedback on my assignments?
+

Yes. AIET is committed to providing meaningful, constructive feedback throughout your studies. Students receive:

  • Personalized feedback on submitted assignments and coursework
  • Guidance on areas of strength and areas for improvement
  • Continuous monitoring of academic progress
  • Instructor support for understanding assessment criteria and expectations

Postgraduate Diploma students also receive supervision and mentoring on their research and dissertation projects from qualified academic supervisors.

Certification & Verification

5 Questions
What certificate or award will I receive upon completion?
+

Upon successfully completing all program requirements, you will be awarded an official AIET certificate or diploma in your chosen area of specialization. The certificate will clearly state:

  • Your full name
  • The specific qualification earned (e.g., Diploma in Educational Leadership)
  • The level of the award (Certificate / Diploma / Higher Diploma / Postgraduate Diploma)
  • Your classification (Distinction / Merit / Pass)
  • Date of completion and award
  • Authorized institutional signatures and official branding
  • A unique Certificate Identification Number for verification
Can my certificate be verified by an employer or institution?
+

Yes. AIET provides a structured manual certificate verification system to allow employers, academic institutions, professional bodies, government agencies, and recruitment firms to confirm the authenticity and validity of any AIET-issued certificate.

To request verification, contact AIET at: info@aietglobal.us

Include the following in your request:

  • Full name of the certificate holder (as it appears on the certificate)
  • Certificate Identification Number (if available)
  • Title and level of the program completed
  • Year or date of completion
  • Purpose of the verification request
Standard processing time: 2–5 working days. AIET is also developing a future digital/QR code-based verification platform for instant online validation.
What happens if a certificate is found to be fraudulent or altered?
+

AIET takes academic fraud extremely seriously. Any form of tampering, alteration, or falsification of an AIET certificate renders it automatically invalid.

If a fraudulent or altered certificate is suspected, it should be reported immediately to info@aietglobal.us with all relevant supporting details. AIET will:

  • Investigate the reported case thoroughly
  • Invalidate the certificate if fraud is confirmed
  • Take appropriate administrative or legal action where applicable
Can I access my academic records after graduation?
+

Yes. AIET provides students with access to their academic records and transcripts where applicable. After graduation, you can contact AIET to request:

  • Official transcripts of your academic performance
  • Confirmation letters for employers or institutions
  • Certificate verification support for job applications or further study admissions

All records are handled securely and confidentially in line with applicable data protection standards.

Does verification of my certificate guarantee employment or university admission?
+

Certificate verification confirms the authenticity of your AIET qualification only. It is important to note that:

  • Verification does not guarantee employment or job placement
  • Verification does not automatically grant professional licensing
  • Verification does not replace independent evaluation by employers or institutions
  • Recognition of qualifications may vary depending on country, employer, or regulatory body

Prospective students are encouraged to verify how AIET qualifications align with their specific career or academic goals before enrolling, especially if they intend to use the qualification for regulated professions.

Accreditation & Recognition

4 Questions
Is AIET accredited?
+

AIET is a legally registered educational institution in the United States, recognized by the Secretary of State of Arizona as a duly registered entity authorized to operate and provide education and training services.

It is important to note that this registration confirms legal establishment and authorization to operate — it is not the same as academic accreditation by a government-recognized accrediting agency. AIET operates as an independent provider of education and professional training.

AIET does not claim degree-granting authority as a university. Its programs are professional-level qualifications designed to support career development and skills enhancement.

Are AIET qualifications recognized internationally?
+

AIET programs are developed using recognized academic frameworks and international best practices in education and training, and are designed to meet international standards. However, formal recognition may vary depending on the country, employer, or institution.

⚠️ Prospective students are strongly advised to verify the recognition requirements within their local jurisdiction or professional body before enrolling, especially if they intend to use the qualification for regulated professions or government employment.

AIET is actively pursuing additional partnerships, affiliations, and recognition opportunities to strengthen the global standing of its qualifications.

Can I use an AIET qualification to pursue further university studies?
+

This depends on the requirements of the receiving institution. While the Postgraduate Diploma is positioned as a bridge to Master's-level study, whether a specific university will recognize it for direct entry into a degree program is entirely at that institution's discretion.

Students intending to use AIET qualifications for progression into degree programs at accredited universities are strongly advised to verify admission requirements of those institutions before enrolling with AIET.

How does AIET ensure quality in its programs?
+

AIET maintains a robust internal quality assurance system that ensures consistency, credibility, and continuous improvement. This includes:

  • Systematic curriculum design and periodic review
  • Clearly defined learning outcomes and competencies per program
  • Standardized assessment frameworks and grading systems
  • Continuous monitoring of teaching and learning effectiveness
  • Regular updates to reflect current trends and industry needs
  • Integration of student feedback into program improvement
  • Strict academic integrity and plagiarism prevention policies

Fees & Payments

4 Questions
How much do AIET programs cost?
+

Program fees vary depending on the level, duration, and specialization of the course. Detailed and up-to-date fee information is provided on the AIET official website and within the Application Portal.

Are payment plans or installments available?
+

Flexible payment options may be available for selected programs. AIET recognizes that learners come from diverse financial backgrounds and aims to make its programs accessible. Contact the admissions team to enquire about available payment arrangements for your chosen program.

What is the refund policy if I withdraw or change my mind?

AIET's refund policy is designed to be fair and transparent:

What payment methods are accepted?

Student Support

5 Questions
What academic support is available to students?
+

AIET provides comprehensive academic support to ensure every student succeeds in their studies:

  • Access to qualified instructors, tutors, and academic mentors
  • Guidance on course content, assignments, and assessments
  • Support in academic writing, referencing, and research skills
  • Personalized and constructive feedback on submitted work
  • Clear communication of course expectations and learning outcomes

AIET ensures that no student is learning in isolation — guidance and support are available at every stage of the journey.

Can I communicate directly with my instructors?

Yes. Students can communicate with instructors and academic staff through AIET's online learning platforms. This includes:

This structured interaction ensures that students receive timely guidance and feel connected to their learning community throughout their program.

What happens if I have technical difficulties with the platform?

AIET provides dedicated technical support to ensure a smooth and uninterrupted learning experience. Technical support includes:

  • Assistance with login access and platform navigation

  • Guidance on using digital tools and learning systems
  • Support with assignment and assessment submission

Contact AIET via email or the official support channel to report technical issues. The team will work promptly to resolve any barriers to your learning.

Are there engagement activities or networking opportunities for students?
+

Yes. AIET promotes an interactive and engaging learning environment even within a fully online framework. Students can participate in:

  • Virtual workshops and professional development training sessions
  • Webinars led by academic and industry professionals
  • Guest lectures and expert discussions
  • Academic discussions, forums, and peer interaction
  • Special networking and professional development events

These activities are designed to help students expand their professional networks, stay current with industry developments, and engage with a vibrant global academic community.

Do I remain connected to AIET after graduation?
+

Yes. AIET actively encourages ongoing alumni engagement. After graduation, you become part of a growing global alumni network with access to:

  • Continued access to learning updates and resources
  • Opportunities to participate in academic and professional activities
  • Inclusion in the expanding AIET alumni community
  • Career tips, industry insights, and professional growth guidance

At AIET, graduation is not the end — it is the beginning of continued professional growth, development, and impact.

Career & Progression

4 Questions
Will an AIET qualification help advance my career?
+

AIET programs are designed with career advancement as a primary objective. They are structured to:

  • Enhance professional skills directly applicable to education and training roles
  • Support career development through practical and industry-relevant learning
  • Improve job performance by deepening expertise in specialized areas
  • Open doors to leadership and senior roles in educational institutions and corporate training environments

Our alumni have gone on to enhance their teaching capabilities, transition into education roles, earn promotions, and take on leadership and administrative positions.

What career roles can AIET graduates pursue?
+

Depending on the level and specialization of qualification, AIET graduates are prepared for a wide range of professional roles, including:

  • Classroom Teacher / Subject Specialist
  • Early Childhood Educator / Nursery/Montessori Teacher
  • School Administrator / Academic Coordinator
  • Education Manager or Director
  • Corporate Trainer / Learning & Development Officer
  • Curriculum Designer / Instructional Developer
  • Special Education Teacher / Inclusion Specialist
  • Education Consultant / Policy Analyst
  • Online Educator / E-Learning Specialist
  • Researcher / Academic Practitioner
Does AIET provide job placement services?
+

AIET does not guarantee or provide direct job placement. However, AIET programs are specifically designed to equip graduates with the skills, knowledge, and qualifications that are directly relevant to the education and training marketplace.

Students also benefit from career-oriented content including career tips for educators, employability guidance, industry trends, and professional growth strategies through the News, Updates & Blog section.

Can I progress to a Master's degree after completing the Postgraduate Diploma?
+

The Postgraduate Diploma is designed as a bridge between professional qualifications and advanced academic study, positioning graduates for Master's-level education. However, admission into a Master's program at a specific university is entirely at that institution's discretion and depends on their individual entry requirements.

AIET encourages Postgraduate Diploma graduates seeking Master's admission to verify the entry requirements of their target institution and present their AIET qualification and portfolio accordingly. Students intending this pathway are advised to clarify this before enrolling.

Policies & Conduct

4 Questions
What is AIET's academic integrity policy?
+

AIET upholds strict standards of academic integrity across all programs. All students are required to:

  • Submit original and authentic work — work must be the student's own
  • Avoid plagiarism in all forms — including copying, paraphrasing without attribution, and submitting work done by others
  • Follow ethical academic and research practices including proper referencing and citation
  • Adhere to the institutional Code of Conduct in all interactions

Violations of academic integrity may result in serious penalties, including failure of the module or program, and in serious cases, disqualification from the institute.

What is the withdrawal policy if I need to leave a program?
+

Students who need to withdraw from a program may do so by formally notifying AIET. Refund eligibility upon withdrawal depends on the applicable refund policy at the time of withdrawal and how much of the program has been accessed or completed.

Students considering withdrawal are encouraged to first contact AIET's student support team to explore alternative options such as a program deferral or pause before making a final decision.

How does AIET handle student data and privacy?
+

AIET is committed to safeguarding all personal and academic data. Key data protection commitments include:

  • All student data is handled securely and confidentially
  • Personal information is not shared beyond the scope of academic administration and verification
  • Certificate verification requests are processed in a controlled and secure manner
  • Data is handled in line with applicable data protection standards

For full details, refer to AIET's Privacy Policy.

How can I provide feedback about my learning experience?
+

AIET values and actively seeks student feedback as a core part of its continuous quality improvement process. Students are provided with:

  • Structured opportunities to provide feedback on programs and services
  • Channels to raise concerns or suggestions directly to the institution

All feedback is taken seriously and feeds directly into the regular review and enhancement of AIET's academic programs and support services. Your experience matters and helps shape the AIET learning environment for future students.
