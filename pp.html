Overview & Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to all personal data processed by the American Institute of Education and Training (AIET) in connection with:

  • Access to and use of the AIET website (aietglobal.us)
  • Submission of applications and enrollment in programs
  • Participation in AIET programs, assessments, and activities
  • Communications and correspondence with AIET staff
  • Certificate verification requests
  • Alumni engagement and post-graduation activities

This policy does not apply to third-party websites or platforms that may be linked from AIET's website. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any external sites you visit.

What Personal Data We Collect

AIET collects the following categories of personal data depending on your interaction with us:

How We Collect Your Data

AIET collects personal data through the following means:

4.1 Directly from You

  • Application forms submitted through the AIET Application Portal
  • Email and telephone communications with AIET staff
  • Contact forms, enquiry submissions, and feedback forms on the website
  • Enrollment and registration processes
  • Participation in assessments, surveys, or academic activities
  • Certificate verification requests submitted by graduates

4.2 Automatically (Website & Platform)

  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies when you visit our website
  • Server log data including IP address, access times, and pages visited
  • Online learning platform usage data such as login times and activity logs

4.3 From Third Parties

  • Background verification organizations (for certificate verification only)
  • Referring educational institutions or partners (where applicable)
  • Payment processing providers (transaction confirmation only — no card details stored by AIET)
5

How We Use Your Personal Data

AIET uses personal data for the following specific and clearly defined purposes:

5.1 Enrollment & Program Administration

  • Processing and evaluating applications for admission
  • Confirming enrollment, issuing login credentials, and onboarding students
  • Delivering program content, assessments, and academic materials
  • Monitoring academic progress and providing academic support
  • Issuing certificates and diplomas upon successful completion

5.2 Communication & Support

  • Responding to enquiries, support requests, and feedback
  • Sending program updates, deadlines, and institutional announcements
  • Notifying students of changes to policies, programs, or services

5.3 Financial Administration

  • Processing program fee payments and managing payment records
  • Administering payment plans and managing outstanding balances
  • Processing refund requests in accordance with our Refund Policy

5.4 Certificate Verification

  • Responding to official verification requests from employers, institutions, or authorized parties
  • Confirming or denying the authenticity of AIET-issued credentials

5.5 Institutional Improvement

  • Analyzing aggregate data to improve program quality and student experience
  • Conducting internal academic quality audits and reviews
  • Developing new programs and improving existing ones

5.6 Legal & Compliance

  • Complying with applicable laws and regulations
  • Investigating and responding to complaints, disputes, or legal claims
  • Maintaining accurate academic and financial records as required

5.7 Marketing & Communications (With Consent)

  • Sending promotional content about new programs, events, or initiatives — only with your consent
  • Sharing alumni success stories (with explicit written permission)
✅ Your Choice: You may opt out of marketing communications at any time by contacting us at [email protected] or clicking the unsubscribe link in any marketing email.
6

Legal Basis for Processing

AIET processes your personal data on the following lawful bases:

  • Contractual Necessity: Processing required to fulfill the enrollment agreement and deliver program services to you
  • Legal Obligation: Processing required to comply with applicable laws, regulations, or lawful court orders
  • Legitimate Interests: Processing for institutional administration, quality assurance, fraud prevention, and security — where your interests are not overridden by these purposes
  • Consent: Processing for marketing communications and optional data uses — where you have freely given explicit consent, which you may withdraw at any time
7

Sharing Your Personal Data

AIET does not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to any third parties for commercial purposes. Your data may be shared in the following limited and controlled circumstances:

7.1 Service Providers

AIET may share data with trusted third-party service providers who assist in operating our services, including:

  • Online learning platform and technology providers
  • Payment processing services (limited to transaction data only)
  • Email communication and customer support platforms
  • Website hosting and data storage providers

All service providers are bound by confidentiality agreements and are only permitted to process data as instructed by AIET.

7.2 Certificate Verification

When a verification request is received via [email protected], AIET may confirm limited certification details — specifically the student's name, program completed, qualification level, and completion status — to the verified requesting party only.

7.3 Legal & Regulatory Requirements

AIET may disclose personal data when required by law, court order, or regulatory authority, or when necessary to protect the rights, safety, or property of AIET or others.

7.4 Academic Fraud Reporting

Where academic fraud or certificate falsification is identified or suspected, AIET reserves the right to share relevant information with appropriate authorities or professional bodies.

⚠ Important: AIET will never disclose your personal data to unauthorized parties, advertisers, or any party that does not have a legitimate and verified need under the conditions described above.
8

International Data Transfers

As a globally operating institution, AIET serves students from countries worldwide. Your personal data may be processed and stored on servers located in the United States or other countries where AIET's service providers operate.

Where data is transferred internationally, AIET takes reasonable steps to ensure that appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data, consistent with applicable data protection laws including, where relevant, GDPR principles for European Economic Area residents.

By enrolling with AIET or using our services, you acknowledge and consent to the transfer of your personal data to the United States and other countries as necessary for the delivery of our services.

9

Data Retention

AIET retains personal data only for as long as is necessary for the purposes for which it was collected, or as required by applicable law.

Data Category Retention Period
Application data (rejected applications) 12 months from application decision
Enrolled student records (academic, financial) 7 years from program completion
Certificate and graduation records Indefinitely (for verification purposes)
Payment and financial records 7 years (legal/accounting requirements)
Communications and correspondence 3 years from last interaction
Marketing preferences Until consent is withdrawn
Website technical/log data Up to 12 months

Upon expiry of the applicable retention period, personal data is securely deleted or anonymized. Certificate and academic records may be retained indefinitely in anonymized or archived form to support ongoing verification services.

10

Cookies & Tracking Technologies

AIET's website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience, analyze website performance, and support our online learning platform.

10.1 Types of Cookies Used

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the website and platform to function properly — these cannot be disabled
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences (e.g., language, login state) to personalize your experience
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our website so we can improve it (data is aggregated and anonymous)
  • Marketing Cookies: Used to track visits to our site and may be used to show you relevant content — only with your consent

10.2 Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of the AIET website and learning platform. For detailed cookie management options, refer to your browser's help documentation.

Data Security

AIET takes the security of your personal data seriously and implements appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect it against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

Our security measures include:

  • Secure, encrypted connections (HTTPS/SSL) for all data transmissions
  • Access controls ensuring data is accessible only to authorized personnel
  • Regular review of data handling and security practices
  • Controlled access to physical and digital systems containing personal data
  • Staff training on data protection and privacy obligations
⚠ Your Responsibility: You are responsible for keeping your login credentials (username and password) secure and confidential. AIET will never ask for your password via email or phone. Report any suspected unauthorized account access immediately to [email protected].

While AIET implements robust security measures, no method of data transmission over the internet is completely secure. In the event of a data breach that poses a risk to your rights and freedoms, AIET will notify affected individuals and relevant authorities as required by applicable law.

13

Minors & Children's Privacy

AIET's programs are primarily designed for adults. The minimum enrollment age for Certificate programs is 16 years, with higher age expectations for advanced programs.

For applicants under the age of 18, AIET requires that applications be submitted and consented to by a parent or legal guardian. Such guardians are jointly responsible for the student's compliance with AIET's Terms of Use and policies.

AIET does not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 16 without verified parental consent. If we become aware that personal data has been collected from a child under 16 without appropriate consent, we will take steps to delete such data promptly.
