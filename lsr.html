Legal Status

A Legally Established Institution

The American Institute of Education and Training (AIET) is a legally established institution registered in the United States. The institute is approved and recognized by the Secretary of State of Arizona as a duly registered entity authorized to operate.

This registration confirms that AIET operates in full compliance with applicable state laws and regulations, is recognized as a legitimate and duly registered organization, and is authorized to provide education and training-related services.

It is important to note that registration with the Secretary of State represents legal establishment and authorization to operate, and not academic accreditation by a government-recognized accrediting agency.

Important Notice to Prospective Students

The American Institute of Education and Training (AIET) operates as an independent education and training provider. AIET qualifications are non-degree programmes, and recognition may vary by country, employer, or institution. Students seeking progression to higher education should verify admission requirements with receiving institutions. You are advised to ensure that AIET programmes meet your individual academic, professional, or regulatory requirements prior to enrollment.
