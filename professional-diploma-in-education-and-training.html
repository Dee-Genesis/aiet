Programme Overview

Advance Your Practice. Deepen Your Impact.

The Professional Diploma in Education and Training at AIET is a comprehensive and career-focused qualification for individuals seeking to develop advanced knowledge, practical teaching skills, and professional competence in the field of education and training.

Building upon foundational concepts, the programme introduces learners to in-depth theories, methodologies, and practices required for effective teaching, training delivery, and educational management. It emphasises a balanced approach to theory and practice — equipping learners to design curriculum, deliver engaging lessons, assess learner performance, and manage diverse learning environments.

The Diploma is designed with flexibility in mind, accommodating both new entrants into education and experienced professionals seeking formal qualification and career advancement. It also serves as a clear progression pathway toward higher-level qualifications.

Programme Structure

Structure & Key Information

Everything you need to know about eligibility, delivery, duration, and credits.

✅

Eligibility Requirements

  • Minimum high school diploma or equivalent qualification
  • Completion of an AIET Certificate Programme
  • Basic proficiency in English (reading, writing, communication)
  • Demonstrated interest or experience in education or training
  • Relevant work experience may be considered on a case-by-case basis
🖥️

Mode of Study

  • Online Learning: Full access to digital materials, lectures, and assessments
  • Blended Learning: Online study combined with structured academic support
  • Guided Learning: Instructor-led sessions, mentoring, and supervision
  • Self-Paced Options: Flexible scheduling within defined timelines
⏱️

Programme Duration

  • Standard Duration: 6 – 9 months
  • Extended Duration: Up to 12 months (depending on pace and specialization)
Standard Extended
🎓

Credit Unit Structure

  • 1 Credit Unit = 10–15 learning hours
  • Total Credit Range: 24–36 Credits
  • Credits include lectures, independent study, assignments, projects, and practical activities

Assessment Framework

How You Are Evaluated

A rigorous, competency-based assessment model designed to evaluate theoretical understanding, practical skill, and real-world professional application.

3-Part
Assessment Competency-Based
Continuous Assessment
30%
Applied / Practical Assessment
40%
Final Assessment
30%

Continuous Assessment (30%)

  • Written assignments and coursework
  • Quizzes and structured tests
  • Case studies and scenario-based tasks
  • Class participation and guided learning activities

Applied / Practical Assessment (40%)

  • Practical projects or applied assignments
  • Instructional design tasks or lesson planning
  • Teaching practice or simulation (where applicable)
  • Workplace-based or case-based activities

Final Assessment (30%)

  • Written examination
  • Final project or portfolio submission
  • Integration of overall programme learning
Grading System

Award Classifications

At Diploma level, students are expected to demonstrate deeper theoretical understanding, analytical ability, and practical competence.

A
Distinction
70% – 100%
Excellent performance with strong understanding, critical thinking, and advanced application of knowledge.
B
Merit
60% – 69%
Good performance with clear understanding and effective application of theories and instructional strategies.
C
Pass
50% – 59%
Satisfactory performance meeting minimum academic, practical, and competency requirements.
F
Fail
Below 50%
Does not meet required standards. Reassessment, resubmission, or additional coursework may be available.

Awards are issued as Diploma with Distinction, Diploma with Merit, or Diploma (Pass). A minimum grade of Pass (C) is required, along with completion of all modules and demonstration of practical teaching or training competence.
